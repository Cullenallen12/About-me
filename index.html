My Favorite Hobby
Playing Sports

I play sports because it brings me enjoyment. Even pros play because they love to play, and when it stops being enjoyable, they retire. We all seek out things that we enjoy doing, and avoid things we do not. Sports also teach you things like teamwork, sportsmanship, and even work on your body to get stronger.
